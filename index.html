Typer

In the web publication Typer the topic of democratization is approached from a typographical perspective. Typer explores what conditions are created by the accessibility of digital tools and how one as designer can facilitate that discussion.

The project seeks to broaden it's scope by engaging established designers and their views on the relation between democratization and the current culture within type design. Participating are Amanda & Erik, Krot & Krass, Parasto Backman and Arina Stoenescu.

The typeface Typer Variable is custom made and uses the publication as its specimen. The font is designed as a variable and adjusted to digital screens.

Typer the web publication
Typer is my bachelor degree project and will be exhibited during Gothenburg design week the fall of 2019.

X-MAS MARKET

Winning visual identity concept for the 2018 Christmas Market at HDK
A visual identity inspired by gift wrapping, the aesthetic of supermarket flyers and sale, in juxtaposition to luxury, sparkle and a ”treat yo self” attitude. Like the christmas market, it’s consumerism with a bit of heart.

Using the components for the digital campaign.

Collaboration with Sara Nygård and Linn Wergelius

Elin Wikberg

Graphic identity for the copywriter Elin Wikberg with aim to showcase her professionally and personally. The inspiration comes from Iittala’s iconic Alvar Aalto Collection, the lived-in colorway and branding of Patagonia and the grainy texture of 70’s album covers and cartoons.

The logo was animated in a forward-moving motion as a metaphor of Elin’s playfulness and go-ahead attitude.

Something Came Over Me

A self-reflecting publication dealing with everyday life procrastination. The layout flirts with typography from rule free fanzines and is bound with dental floss. All of this gathered in a book the size of fits-in-your-small-bag for last minute reading.

Approximately half of the student population and 15%–20% of the adult population are presumed having substantial difficulties due to chronic and recurrent procrastination in their everyday life.

Edition of 6
135x205 mm
Insert 24p 4+4
Cover 4p 2+2
Bound with floss
Printed at Akademin Valand
